WHO WE ARE

Digital Apprenticeship Program.

Sasakazi addresses barriers to decent employment, including the availability of skilled employees among the youth and the demand by businesses to hire. The platform occupies a central position between MSMEs and the tech talent pool, providing support to young Tech Professionals in acquiring practical work experience and launching careers in the technology sector. This is achieved through professional digital apprenticeships, job placement assistance, training on soft skills, and one-on-one mentorship by experienced professionals from the tech industry.

WHAT WE DO

Digital Apprenticeship Programme

The Sasakazi platform aims to disrupt the industry by offering a professional digital apprenticeship program that combines time-tested soft skills training with a focus on digital work settings. Additionally, Sasakazi supports MSMEs in their digitization efforts. With the platform, junior tech talents now have the opportunity to showcase their qualifications and connect with potential opportunities and employers.

Job Matching

We collaborate with employers on their digital projects, identify gaps, and connect them with skilled tech talents from our vetted pool.

Digital Transformation

We discover and partner with businesses with digital needs, matching them with tech talent to deliver digital solutions to their operations.

Training & Upskilling

We provide young tech professionals with work experience and resources to upscale their skills through real project sites and partnerships.
